### Scope
Complete AML/KYC initiation workflow triggered by natural language command (e.g., "Initiate AML/KYC for Acme Capital LLC"). Covers client lookup, corporate tree resolution, party scoping, conflicts-based risk screening via Moody's GRID, risk scoring, match acceptance, and ongoing monitoring setup.

### Goals
- Automate end-to-end AML/KYC initiation with minimal manual intervention
- Ensure comprehensive corporate structure screening with user-controlled scope
- Deliver risk-scored results with clear HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW event classification
- Establish ongoing monitoring and schedule periodic reviews

- One entry in `{moodysHitEvents}` per `searchParty`

- `summary.partialWeightEvents` тАФ deduplicated event codes across all `partialWeight` hits

#### B. Party-Level Risk Score Calculation

#### B. Party-Level Risk Score Calculation

Evaluate in order, stop at first match:

1. If any Critical code appears in `summary.fullWeightEvents` тЖТ score = 100, ЁЯФ┤ High
2. If any Critical code appears in `summary.partialWeightEvents` тЖТ score = 10, ЁЯФ┤ High
3. Otherwise, sum all unique event code values across `summary.fullWeightEvents` (100%) and `summary.partialWeightEvents` (50%, rounded down)

| Tier | Codes | Full Weight | Partial Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Critical | BLK, BRB, BUS, DEN, FOF, FOS, FRD, IRC, MLA, ORG, PEP, REG, SEC, SEX, SNX, TER, WLT | 100 тЖТ instant ЁЯФ┤ High | 10 тЖТ instant ЁЯФ┤ High |
| Valuable | CFT, CYB, DTF, ENV, FUG, GAM, HUM, IMP, KID, LMD, LNS, MOR, MSB, MUR, OBS, PRJ, RES, SMG, SPY, TAX, TFT, TRF, VCY | 5 | 2 |
| Investigative | ARS, AST, BUR, CON, DPS, FOR, IGN, PSP, ROB | 3 | 1 |
| Probative | ABU, CPR, IPR, MIS, NSC | 1 | 0 |

| Risk Level | Score |
|---|---|
| ЁЯФ┤ High | тЙе 10 |
| ЁЯЯа Medium | 4 тАУ 9 |
| ЁЯФШ Low | 0 тАУ 3 |

`noWeight.riskEvents` do not contribute to score.

#### C. Overall Risk Score Calculation

Start with the client party's `riskScore` as the baseline. Then for each related party, add a contribution based on their `riskLevel`:

| Related Party Risk Level | Points Added |
|---|---|
| ЁЯФ┤ High | 10 |
| ЁЯЯа Medium | 5 |
| ЁЯФШ Low | 1 |

Overall score = `client riskScore` + sum of all related party contributions, capped at 100.

| Overall Risk | Score |
|---|---|
| ЁЯФ┤ High | тЙе 67 |
| ЁЯЯа Medium | 34 тАУ 66 |
| ЁЯФШ Low | 0 тАУ 33 |

### Appendix F. Multi-Source Relationship Rule

Relationship buckets are **non-exclusive** тАФ a single entity can and should be recorded
in multiple buckets simultaneously. Evaluate all bucket conditions for every entity
regardless of source; do not stop at the first match.

**Example:** The GUO is always also a Parent. If it additionally appears in the
shareholders list, it holds three relationships at once: `["GUO", "Parent", "Shareholder"]`.
It appears as a single row in `{relatedPartiesTable[]}` with all three labels тАФ never
as duplicate rows, and never counted more than once toward any bucket's `total`.
